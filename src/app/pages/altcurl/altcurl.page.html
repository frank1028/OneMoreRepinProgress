Alternate Curl
instructions

Targets Outer & Inner Bicep Heads
1. Hold a pair of dumbbells at arm length by your sides, with thumbs forward and resting against the inside of the plates.

2. While keeping your upper arms vertical curl one dumbbell while rotating it until your palm faces upward.

3. Lower the dumbbell, and repeat with the opposite arm.
